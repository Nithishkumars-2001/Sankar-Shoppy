About

Sankar Shoppy has been a trusted name in the world of traditional Tamil household essentials for more than 7+ successful years. We bring purity, quality and tradition together, offering products that maintain the cultural beauty of every home in Tamil Nadu. As a dedicated Shopping & Retail brand, we make sure our customers get the finest kolam solutions with reliable service.

Our main focus is on providing Colour & White Kolamavu made from high-quality, smooth and long-lasting powder. Along with this, we offer beautifully designed Kolam Stickers, ready-to-use Kolam Kits, festival special rangoli sets, and daily-use kolam materials perfect for homes, shops, apartments, and traditional functions. Every product is crafted to give a clean, neat, and bright kolam finish.

At Sankar Shoppy, we proudly deliver products across all districts of Tamil Nadu, ensuring fast and secure packing. Whether you are a homemaker, shop owner, event organizer, or kolam enthusiast, we provide everything you need to create beautiful traditional and modern kolam designs. With high customer satisfaction and consistent quality, our business continues to grow stronger every year.
