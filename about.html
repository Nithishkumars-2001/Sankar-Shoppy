WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Sankar Shoppy Your One-Stop Kolam Store

Sankar Shoppy has been a trusted name in traditional Tamil household essentials for more than 7+ successful years. We bring purity, quality, and tradition together by offering products that preserve the cultural beauty of every home in Tamil Nadu. As a dedicated shopping and retail brand, we ensure our customers always receive the finest kolam solutions with reliable and friendly service.

Founder Name
Founder Name - Chandmaliknaseer

Our main focus is on providing high-quality Colour and White Kolamavu made from smooth, long-lasting powder for a perfect and clean finish. In addition, we offer beautifully designed kolam stickers, ready-to-use kolam kits, festival-special rangoli sets, and daily-use kolam materials suitable for homes, shops, apartments, and traditional functions. Each product is carefully selected to deliver a bright, neat, and professional kolam look every time.
